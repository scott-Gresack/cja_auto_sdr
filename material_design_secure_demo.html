‚ùå Security Anti-Patterns to Avoid

Anti-Pattern Risk Solution
Storing passwords in plaintext Critical - Data breach Use bcrypt/argon2 hashing
Exposing sensitive paths in frontend High - Information disclosure Never hardcode user/system paths
