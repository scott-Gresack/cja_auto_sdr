Common Use Cases

Scenario 1: Dynamic B2B Ordering

Customer: Enterprise procurement team needing inventory replenishment with custom terms.

  1. Discovery: Agent locates pricing, inventory, fulfillment services
  2. Context: Pulls customer segment (Enterprise), LTV, and historical preferences
  3. Composition: Assembles customized offer with volume discount, payment terms, delivery windows
  4. Orchestration: Routes through approval workflow, sends to ERP, activates notifications
  5. Attribution: Tracks engagement and conversion touchpoints end-to-end

Scenario 2: Real-Time Cross-Sell

Trigger: Customer adds high-margin item to cart.

  1. Recommendation Engine: Identifies complementary products with 82% co-purchase correlation
  2. Inventory Check: Validates availability at preferred fulfillment location
  3. Offer Engine: Calculates optimal discount to maximize basket size
  4. Compliance: Verifies promotion eligibility and budget limits
  5. Activation: Shows suggestion on web, SMS follow-up, email campaign queued

Scenario 3: Retention at Risk

Condition: Customer inactive for 60+ days despite high historical AOV.

  1. Segmentation: Flagged as high-value, at-risk, retention-sensitive
  2. Personalization: Generates product recommendations based on purchase history
  3. Offer Strategy: Dynamic retention offer (special discount or loyalty bonus)
  4. Channel Selection: Email primary, SMS secondary, in-app tertiary
  5. Measurement: Tracks re-engagement rate and lift attribution
